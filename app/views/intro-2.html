Why is it better to use directives?

  • Betters Separation of concerns
  • Reusability: (standalone controls, reusable modifiable HTML element behavior)
  • Much more declarative, cleaner HTML
  • Fits well into the MVC philosophy. Do I really need to sell the values of MVC?
  • Mode solid JS (no selectors)
  • The cool factor of creating your own html elements or modifiying the behavior of existing ones at a low level
  • Less code (but not always)
  • 80% of Angular is a set of directives (ng-controller anyone? ng-app, ng-click, ng-show, etc...), understanding directives will make you a better Angular developer.

Drawbacks

  • Directives can be f'n hard
  • Like blackmagic sometimes
  • You may have to learn/understand low level DOM web stuff
  • Not the best documentation out there
